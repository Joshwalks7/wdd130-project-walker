Welcome to Brick Con 2025

Brick Con 2025 is a free family-friendly event for LEGO enthusiasts of all ages! It's an opportunity for LEGO lovers in the community of Rexburg, Idaho to come together and appreciate the work of others and bond over this fun hobby. There will be a kids section, a giant convention for builders to display custom made MOCs (my own creation), famous LEGO influencers speaking, and an auction.
