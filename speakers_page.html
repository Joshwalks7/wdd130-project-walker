Brick Con 2025 Speakers!

This year we are pleased to hear from Ryan McCullough (MandRproductions), David Hall (Solid Brix Studios), and Jhae (RichboyJhae)! These are some of the most famous content creators covering LEGO news, product reviews, and building incredible MOCs. They will each be given 15 minutes to speak with us. You don’t want to miss it.


Ryan McCullough

Ryan is a famous YouTuber known for his set reviews, vlogs, weekly Q and A’s and extensive knowledge of rumored sets. He is one of the first content makers on the platform!

David Hall

David has also been in the LEGO community for a long time. He was one of the original content creators on YouTube uploading videos of MOCs and reviewing LEGO sets. He also owns one of the biggest LEGO minifigure armies.

Jhae

Jhae has been known in the LEGO community as one of the best MOC builders. He has recreated some of the biggest and most complex scenes from the Star Wars universe. No one doubts his ability and dedication to the craft.
